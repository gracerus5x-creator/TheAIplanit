## Primary inbox

**info@theaiplanit.com**

This goes to the editorial team. Expect a reply within **2 business days** (Lagos time, UTC+1).

## What to send where

| What you have | Use |
|---|---|
| **Factual correction or update** on an existing article | info@theaiplanit.com — subject: "Correction: [article title]" |
| **Tip about a new AI tool we should review** | info@theaiplanit.com — subject: "Tip: [tool name]" |
| **Affiliate / partnership proposal** | info@theaiplanit.com — subject: "Partnership" |
| **Press / interview request** | info@theaiplanit.com — subject: "Press" |
| **Privacy concern or data-deletion request** | info@theaiplanit.com — subject: "Privacy" (see our [Privacy Policy](/privacy/)) |

## Office location

Operated remotely from **Lagos, Nigeria** (West Africa, UTC+1).

## Response time expectations

- **Editorial inquiries:** within 2 business days
- **Privacy / data-deletion requests:** within 30 days (legal max under GDPR/NDPR)
- **Partnership / affiliate proposals:** within 5 business days, sometimes longer during product reviews

## What we won't reply to

- Mass cold pitches with no relevance to AI tools or SaaS
- Requests to "publish this article we wrote for backlinks"
- Demands for free product placement disguised as editorial

If your message gets no reply within the windows above and isn't in that "won't reply" bucket, please follow up once — sometimes things get caught in filters.
