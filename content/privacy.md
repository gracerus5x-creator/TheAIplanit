## Quick summary

The AI Planit is a content website. We do not require accounts, do not store credit-card info, and do not sell personal data. Below is the full breakdown of what we collect, what for, and how to control it.

## Who we are

**The AI Planit** (theaiplanit.com), operated by Augustine Usifo, Lagos, Nigeria.
Privacy contact: **info@theaiplanit.com**

## What we collect

### 1. Anonymous analytics
If we use a privacy-respecting analytics tool (e.g. Google Analytics, Cloudflare Web Analytics, Plausible), it logs:
- Pages visited
- General location (country / region — never street-level)
- Browser type and screen size
- Referring website (where you came from)

This data is **aggregated and anonymous**. It cannot be tied back to your identity.

### 2. Affiliate-link click attribution
When you click an affiliate link, the destination merchant may set a tracking cookie identifying The AI Planit as the referrer. **We do not control this cookie** and do not receive details of what you bought — only the commission credit.

### 3. Voluntary contact
If you email us at info@theaiplanit.com, we receive the email and any info you choose to include. We retain emails for support purposes only and never share them with third parties.

## What we do NOT collect

- We do not have user accounts or logins.
- We do not store credit-card or payment information (purchases happen on merchant sites, not here).
- We do not run pop-up surveys, exit-intent forms, or session-replay tools.

## Your rights

Under the EU **GDPR**, the UK **DPA**, the Nigerian **NDPR**, and similar laws:

- **Access:** You may ask what data we hold on you. Reply within 30 days.
- **Deletion:** You may ask us to delete data we hold. Done within 30 days unless legal retention applies.
- **Opt-out of analytics:** Most browsers offer a "Do Not Track" signal — we honor it. You can also use browser extensions like uBlock Origin or Privacy Badger.
- **Cookie control:** Clear or block cookies in your browser settings.

To exercise any of these, email **info@theaiplanit.com** with the subject "Privacy request".

## Children

The AI Planit is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have, contact us and we will delete it immediately.

We will date-stamp the bottom of this page whenever we update it. Material changes will be flagged at the top for 30 days.
