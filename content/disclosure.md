## Plain-English summary

**Some of the links on this site are affiliate links.** When you click one and buy something, we may earn a commission — at **no extra cost to you**. This commission helps fund the time spent researching, testing, and updating our reviews.

We are required to disclose this by the U.S. Federal Trade Commission (FTC), the UK Advertising Standards Authority (ASA), and similar bodies globally. We are happy to — it keeps us honest.

## Programs we participate in

The AI Planit is a participant in (or has applied to) the following affiliate networks:

- **Impact.com** — covering tools like Claude, Jasper, Notion, Grammarly, and others
- **ShareASale** — covering SEMrush, ConvertKit, Teachable, and similar
- **Amazon Associates** — covering related books, accessories, and hardware
- **Direct partnerships** with individual SaaS vendors when offered

The list above is updated as we join new networks. Specific vendors may change.

## How affiliate commission affects our coverage

It **does not affect our ratings**. Our editorial process:

1. We pick tools to review based on what readers ask about and what's genuinely useful — not by commission rate.
2. A tool's commission rate is invisible to the editor writing the review.
3. We compare every recommended tool against realistic alternatives, including free and lower-commission options.
4. When a tool stops being worth recommending, we update or pull the article — even if it had been a top earner.

## Cookie-based tracking

When you click an affiliate link, the destination merchant typically sets a cookie identifying The AI Planit as the referrer. This cookie usually expires 1–90 days later, depending on the program. We do not see the contents of your purchase — only that a referral attribution occurred.

You can clear or block these cookies in your browser settings without affecting your ability to use this site.

## Editorial independence

- We do **not** accept paid placements disguised as reviews.
- We do **not** accept money to remove negative coverage.
- We do **not** write reviews for products we have not used or, at minimum, evaluated through demos, documentation, and third-party reports.
