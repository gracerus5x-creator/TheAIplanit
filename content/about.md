## What this site is

**The AI Planit** is an independent review hub for AI tools and SaaS — comparisons, deep-dives, and buyer's guides for builders, marketers, operators, and curious professionals trying to separate genuine productivity from hype.

We test what we recommend. When a tool changes pricing, drops a feature, or ships a real upgrade, our reviews get updated. When a tool stops being worth the money, we say so.

## Who we are

**The AI Planit Editorial** is an independent editorial team building no-nonsense reviews of AI tools and SaaS for working professionals. We are part of a broader independent content portfolio focused on technology, wellness, and personal finance.

## How we choose what to review

- **Practical first.** If a tool doesn't help someone actually ship work, we usually skip it — no matter how viral the launch.
- **Pricing transparency.** Every review states the current price tier and any catches (annual lock-ins, hidden usage fees, etc).
- **Comparison over isolation.** A 10/10 review of a single tool tells you nothing. We compare against the realistic alternatives.

## Editorial independence

- We accept **affiliate commissions** from some tools we cover — fully disclosed on our [Affiliate Disclosure](/disclosure/) page.
- We do **not** accept payment to write positive reviews. A tool's commission rate has no influence on its rating.
- We do **not** publish sponsored posts disguised as editorial.
- If we change our mind on a tool, the article gets updated with a dated note explaining what changed.

## A note on AI-assisted writing

Some of our drafts are produced with AI assistance and then edited and fact-checked by a human before publication. We do not publish AI output unedited. If you spot a factual error, please [contact us](/contact/) — we fix corrections within 48 hours and add a dated correction note to the article.
